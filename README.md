# FUTURE_DS_01
# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a retail sales dataset to uncover business insights related to sales, profit, customer segments, products, and regional performance. The workflow includes data cleaning, SQL-based analysis, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Dataset
* Superstore Sales Dataset
* Contains order, customer, product, sales, profit, discount, and regional information.

## Tools & Technologies
* Python (Pandas)
* PostgreSQL (SQL)
* Power BI
* Git & GitHub

## Project Workflow
1. Data Cleaning using Pandas
   * Checked missing values
   * Removed duplicates
   * Converted date columns
   * Created Year and Month features

2. SQL Analysis
   * Overall sales, profit, and quantity analysis
   * Category-wise and sub-category analysis
   * Product performance
   * Region and state analysis
   * Customer segment analysis
   * Monthly and yearly sales trends
   * Discount and profit analysis
     
3. Power BI Dashboard
   * KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Quantity)
   * Sales Trend
   * Category & Region Analysis
   * Top Products
   * Customer Segment Analysis
   * Profit vs Discount Scatter Plot
   * Interactive Filters

## Key Insights
* Identified top-performing categories and products.
* Compared regional sales and profitability.
* Analyzed the impact of discounts on profit.
* Explored customer segment performance.
* Discovered monthly and yearly sales trends.

## Key Findings & Business Insights

* The highest total sales were recorded in **2017**, generating **$733215.25** in revenue.
* The highest total profit was achieved in **2017**, with a profit of **$93439.25**.
* **Technology** was the best-performing product category in terms of total sales.
* **Phones** generated the highest overall profit, while **Fasteners** recorded the lowest profit.
* **West** contributed the highest sales and profitability among all regions.
* **California** emerged as the top-performing state based on total sales.
* **Consumer** generated the highest revenue, indicating it is the company's most valuable customer segment.
* **Canon Copier** was the highest-selling product, whereas **Eureka Disposable Bags** recorded the lowest profitability.
* Analysis of discounts showed that higher discounts were generally associated with lower profits, with several highly discounted orders resulting in losses.
* Monthly sales trends revealed stronger sales during **November**, indicating seasonal demand patterns.

### Conclusion

The analysis identified the key drivers of revenue and profitability across products, regions, customer segments, and time. The interactive Power BI dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify high-performing areas, and make data-driven decisions regarding pricing, inventory, and regional sales strategies.
